# 🎉 Congratulations: High-Level Design (HLD) Complete!

## Summary of Your HLD Journey
You have mastered the **Building Blocks** of high-scale systems:

1.  **System Basics:** Scalability, Reliability, Availability.
2.  **CAP Theorem:** Picking your battles (Consistency vs. Availability).
3.  **Load Balancer:** The Manager who keeps servers from catching fire.
4.  **Consistent Hashing:** The Ring that makes scaling easy.
5.  **Caching (Redis):** The "Front Desk" for high-speed data.
6.  **Database Scaling:** Sharding (Splitting) and Replication (Copying).
7.  **API Gateway:** The "Receptionist" of Microservices.
8.  **CDN:** Moving data physically closer to users.
9.  **Message Queues (Kafka):** Asynchronous buffers for big traffic.
10. **Rate Limiting:** Protecting your site from hackers and bots.

---

## 🏋️ Main Components of HLD
*   **Load Balancer:** Spreading the upload traffic.
*   **S3:** For storing photos.
*   **Redis:** For the feed cache.
*   **CDN:** For loading photos fast in different cities.

---

## 🎤 Ultimate HLD Interview Strategy
1.  **Listen to requirements first:** Is it for 1,000 users or 1 Billion?
2.  **Draw the HLD diagram first:** Boxes (React, API Gateway, Redis, DB).
3.  **Explain the "Why":** Why Redis? Why Kafka? (Always use the analogies: Pizza delivery, Warehouse, Library).
